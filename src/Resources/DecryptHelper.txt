----------------------------------
|       Arc4u Cyphertool.        |
----------------------------------

Let you decrypt a text or a file using a certificate or a pfx file.

With a certificate in the certificate store or keychain.
arc4u.cyphertool decrypt from text -c devCertName text "cypher text"

With a pfx file.
arc4u.cyphertool decrypt from pfx -c "C:\temp\devCert.pfx" -p password text "cypher text"

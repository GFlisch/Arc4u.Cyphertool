----------------------------------
|       Arc4u Cyphertool.        |
----------------------------------

Let you encrypt a text or a file using a certificate or a pfx file.

With a certificate in the certificate store or keychain.
arc4u.cyphertool encrypt from text -c devCertName text ""clear text""

With a pfx file.
arc4u.cyphertool encrypt from pfx -c ""C:\temp\devCert.pfx"" -p password text ""clear text""

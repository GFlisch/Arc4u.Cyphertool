----------------------------------
|       Arc4u Cyphertool.        |
----------------------------------

Let you encrypt a text or a file using a certificate or a pfx file.

With a certificate in the certificate store or keychain.
arc4u.cyphertool encrypt from certificatestore "certifcate friendly name" text "clear text"

With a pfx file.
arc4u.cyphertool encrypt from pfx "C:\temp\devCert.pfx" -p password text "clear text"
